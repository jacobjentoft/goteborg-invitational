The venue

Sankt Jörgen Park

We’re staying on-site all weekend. Golf by day, resort life by night. No excuses.

01

The Course

18 holes of manicured parkland just outside Göteborg. Fresh air, tidy fairways, birdsong — and, per tradition, plenty of double bogeys.

02

The Pool Club

Heated pool, cabanas, and zero wind. Official recovery station for wounded egos and sunburnt forearms.

03

Spa & Winery

Yes, the resort has its own winery. No, that is not a valid excuse for Saturday’s scores. The spa is for whoever three-putts the most.

Inez & Ernst

The restaurant where the day’s leaderboard gets disputed nightly. Bring receipts, scorecards, and witnesses.
