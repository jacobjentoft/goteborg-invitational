After golf

The 19th hole

Gothenburg after dark: rooftops in the sun, terraces at golden hour, clubs after midnight. Scores are settled at Inez & Ernst — everything after that is settled here.

  • 🏟️ Gothia Cup starts July 12 — the world’s biggest youth football tournament. The city fills up on our exact weekend; expect lively streets and queues.
  • ☀️ Sunset ~22:00. Strategy: start on rooftops in daylight, descend into the clubs later.
  • 🚶 Andra & Tredje Långgatan go pedestrian in summer, terraces spilling into the street — the pre-party zone.
  • 💳 ~90–120 SEK a beer, 145–175 a cocktail, 100–200 club entry. Card-only city — leave the cash.
  • 🪪 Some Avenyn clubs run 23–25 age minimums on weekends. Bring ID. For once, we pass comfortably.

Night 1 · Friday

Ease in

Johanna or Bar Himmel rooftop → Järntorget → Bongo → Barabicu / Zamenhof → Nefertiti for live music.

Low door-drama, high fun, good value. Round 1 grievances aired on the terraces.

Night 2 · Saturday

The big one

Sunset at Above or Cielo → cocktails at Puta Madre or Stranger → Yaki-Da, or Lounge(s) for everything under one roof.

Smart-casual, IDs out, arrive before 23:00 to skip the worst queues. Konrad enters to his anthem.

Night 3 · Sunday

Wind down

Långgatorna terraces in afternoon sun → pints at Ölstugan Tullen → Ivans in Ringön if legs remain.

The champion buys. The city is full of youth football teams with our exact energy level: gone.

⚠️ Avenyn door policy is a lottery. Entry fees around 200 SEK and bouncers who invent reasons at will, even with guest lists and ID. Go smart-casual, arrive sober-looking, and always have a backup venue in the same zone. Door diplomacy: The Keeper.
